The Fellowship Program

Are you a female or a nonbinary individual who is interested in learning how to code, meet like-minded individuals from around the world, and use your skills to help advance humanity?

We're excited to announce the Elevate Tech Fellowship! In this 3 month long program, your team will receive a $100 grant to develop a social good project with technology. Along the way, you'll have access to workshops, panels, resources, and mentors to help you.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN?

  • Learn how to code a website or app
  • Make connections with young female and nonbinary leaders in STEM from around the world
  • Receive a Certificate of Excellence to boost your resume
  • Receive funding and resources to fund your tech social good project
  • Learn what it's like to major in tech and work at tech companies from certified girl bosses
  • Receive priority registration for our future events (Elevate Hacks II!)
  • Become an alumni member in our Discord community and provide guidance to new fellows in future cohorts
