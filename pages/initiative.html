Elevate Hacks
Elevate Hacks was an international virtual hackathon for female identifying and non-binary students by Elevate Tech. We welcomed students with a variety of different skills and backgrounds from all over the world to come together and attend engaging workshops, learn new technical skills, and create impactful projects.

Intro to UI/UX Series
We invited web designers to host 2 workshops teaching UI/UX principles and how to create designs using Figma and Canva.
Fellwoship Program
In this 3 month long program, your team will receive a $100 grant to develop a social good project with technology. Along the way, you'll have access to workshops, panels, resources, and mentors to help you.
